Decently Yummy Burgers

Back to main page

So, ever go over to a buddies for BBQ burgers and he/she hands you a plate just crumbles? Kinda like a sad little pile of loose ground beef, but with some chunks? Or maybe it just gets split in half real funny and then loses a small chunk of the side somewhere? Me too.

Too. Damn. Often.

Or maybe my friends are just idiots.

Well, Im gonna tell you how to avoid that with these simple ingredients:

  • 2 lb Ground Beef
  • 1 Egg, Beaten
  • 3/4 Cup Dried Breadcrumbs
  • 3 tbps Evaporated Milk
  • 2 tbps Worcestershire Sauce
  • 1/8 tbps Cayenne Pepper
  • 2 Clove Garlic, Minced

I got my ingredients. Now what?

Uh, I dunno. Mix em up and shape them into patties perhaps? This Recipe calls for making 8 patties out of this. Id honestly just go for 4 nice big boys. Also, consider adding Paprika, Diced Onion and some Tabasco Sauce to the mix. And be forewarned. If your crack this recipe out at the next neighborhood BBQ, there a real chance the BBQ boys/girls may seriously envy you. And a real chance their significant others may seriously covet you. Not that this is a bad thing per se, its just, 2 mortal sins at the BBQ-get-together is kinda rookie numbers. Any self respecting, worldly individual should be going for 5 or more.

Burgers. Mash up, shape em into patties, toss em on the grill and bam. Shortly thereafter, you got an America right there on a plate!
